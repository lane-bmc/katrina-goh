RCAC Winter Classic 2017

After a two year hiatus, the RCAC Winter Classic is back! Join us for an awesome night of puck-shooting, stick-tapping, and fellowshipping, this time in a bigger gym! We'd love for you to come play with us!

Kerrisdale Community Centre
5851 W Boulevard, Vancouver
December 10th
8:30-10:30PM
$5 admission

Here are a few things you need to know and remember about the night:

Sign up!

To ensure a good amount of playing time and fun for everyone, we need to make sure that people sign up and we know how many players there will be. There are a limited number of spots, so please sign up!

Sign Up!

Bring $5!

Because we're renting out a community centre gymnasium this time, please bring $5 to help cover the cost of the rental and rental insurance.

Arrive early!

Our 8:30-10:30pm time slot includes setup and cleanup. In order to maximize our time and have things move smoothly, please arrive early - come at 8:15 if you can!

Bring protective gear!

We recommend at least some knee pads and gloves, but bring whatever you think you'll need to stay safe. It's all for fun, so we don't want any injuries!

No slapshots!

For the safety of everyone involved, there will be no wind-up slapshots. Keep your stick below your waist at all times - there are other people playing too!

Plastic blades please!

If you plan to bring your own stick, great! (If you can't, don't worry - just let us know in the sign-up form and we'll try to bring one for you). Please bring a plastic-bladed stick if you can, and if you must bring a wooden-bladed stick, then please remove any tape and tape residue on the blade. We don't want to mark up their floors!

Stay hydrated!

Bring water and drink regularly!

Have fun!

Get to know some people in your church family that you might not know too well, and have fun and fellowship with one another through a great night of hockey!

If that all sounds good to you, then let's play together!
